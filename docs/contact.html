Data usage and Disclaimer

For the benefit of the wider biomedical community, all data here are open and available on github . Users can freely download and search the data, and we encourage the use and publication of results generated from these data. There are absolutely no restrictions or embargoes on the publication of results derived from ES-NDD data. However, ES-NDD aggregates data from many different other resources, thus, there might be inconsistencies around versions. Please contact us with any concerns, we will do our best to address them.

Disclaimer: Please note that all content on this website is provided for academic and research purposes only. It has not undergone clinical validation, and should not be used for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment
